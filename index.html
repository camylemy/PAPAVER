Our Story

Papa Ver’s Bundatan is an eatery that sells foods such as silog, mami, pares, rice meal, and more. The business started in April 25, 2022, and is currently located at 2531 San Pedro St. Pinoy Cor. Balut, Tondo, Manila. According to the business’ owner, Mark Clemente, its name was derived from his late father’s name, Vergilio Clemente. His father was the one who encouraged him to establish a business, and by the concept of Filipinos loving rice, this gave him the idea to start an eatery.
