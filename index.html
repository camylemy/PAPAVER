PAPA VER'S BUNDATAN

ABOUT US

Papa Ver’s Bundatan started in April 25, 2022, and is currently located at 2531 San Pedro St. Balut, Tondo, Manila. According to the business’ owner, Mark Clemente, its name was derived from his loving father’s name. Mr. Clemente started thinking about how much people in the country love rice, which became the basis for his idea. At first, the eatery was not very popular until it continued and gained recognition. Papa Ver's Bundatan is a small eatery that often goes unnoticed due to its limited space. However, despite this, the business thrives as customers consistently return. It's not just the tasty food that keeps them coming back; the excellent customer service offered by the staff also plays a significant role. The inviting ambiance and personalized care ensure that customers feel valued and appreciated, creating a special bond that makes them want to return time and time again.
