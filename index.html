Our Story

Papa Ver’s Bundatan is an eatery that sells foods such as silog, mami, pares, rice meals, sizzling, short orders, and drinks. The business started on April 25, 2022, and is currently located at 2531 San Pedro St. Pinoy Cor. Balut, Tondo, Manila. According to the business’ owner, Mark Clemente, the one who encouraged him to establish a business was his late father. By the concept of Filipinos loving rice, this gave him the idea to start an eatery.
