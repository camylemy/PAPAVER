Our Story

Papa Ver’s Bundatan is an eatery that sells foods such as silog, mami, pares, rice meals, sizzling, short orders, and drinks. The business started on April 25, 2022, and is currently located at 2531 San Pedro St. Pinoy Cor. Balut, Tondo, Manila. According to the business’ owner, Mark Clemente, the one who encouraged him to establish a business was his late father. By the concept of Filipinos loving rice, this gave him the idea to start an eatery.

WHY CHOOSE PAPA VER'S BUNDATAN?

In Papa Ver's Bundatan, you can order different kinds of meals such as silog, mami, pares (pares overload, lechon pares, beef pares), rice meals (fried chicken, tapa w/ egg, fried liempo, chicharon bulaklak w/ rice, fried porkchop), sizzling (liempo, pork chop, chicken), short orders (chicharon bulaklak, pork sisig, lechon kawali), drinks, and extras (rice and egg).

WHAT MAKES OUR EATERY SPECIAL?

The overall atmosphere in Papa Ver’s Bundatan suggests a casual dining experience, offering meals that are good value for money which caused customers to come back.
